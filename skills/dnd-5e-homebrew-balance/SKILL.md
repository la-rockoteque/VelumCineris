# DND 5e Homebrew Balance

Balance homebrew content with explicit tradeoff analysis.

## Follow This Workflow

1. Identify intended tier, role, and comparison baselines from official 5e patterns.
2. Compare offensive output, defensive profile, utility breadth, and action economy.
3. Detect runaway scaling, stacked advantage loops, and low-cost hard control.
4. Propose minimal changes first, then optional stronger redesign alternatives.
5. Provide a playtest checklist with expected outcomes and failure signals.

## Balance Lenses

- Action economy pressure per round
- Resource cost versus payoff
- Reliability and save/attack gating
- Encounter-warping utility outside combat
- Multiclass and feature-combo abuse risk

## Guardrails

- Avoid absolute claims; provide relative baseline comparisons.
- Preserve original fantasy and identity while tuning numbers/mechanics.
- Distinguish rules defects from balance defects.
