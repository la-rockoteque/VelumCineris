# Scrolling

Horizontal scroll belongs to the content region, not the page. Keep tool chrome fixed and push overflow into the local data container.

## Contract

- Use local overflow for wide datasets and previews.
- Preserve surrounding actions, filters, and headings outside the scroll region.
- Make the scroll boundary visually clear.
