# Feats

## Threshold Touched
*Prerequisite: Wisdom 13 or higher*
You can interfere with planar movement near you.
### Prevent Teleport
When a creature within 10 feet attempts to teleport you may use your reaction to negate the effect.
### Threshold Sense
You have advantage on Wisdom checks to detect planar effects.

---

## Veil-Breath Adept
*Prerequisite: Exposure to the Veil*
You can survive and function within the Veil without protective equipment.
### Veil Adaptation
You can breathe and survive in the Veil without the need for a spacesuit or sealed environment.
### Environmental Immunity
You are immune to suffocation and atmospheric pressure effects caused by the Veil.

---

## Polyglot Of The Trade
You have learned the common transactional tongues of the world.
### Trade Pidgin Fluency
You can speak read and write Trade Pidgin.
### Marillic Fluency
You can speak read and write Marillic.

---

## Strength Manifest
Your physical force expresses itself with certainty.
### Forceful Presence
You count as one size larger when determining carrying capacity and pushing dragging or lifting.
### Braced Might
You have advantage on Strength checks made to resist being moved.

---

## Dexterity Manifest
Your movements resolve with unnatural precision.
### Reactive Footing
You gain a +1 bonus to initiative rolls.
### Controlled Motion
You ignore difficult terrain during the first 10 feet of movement each turn.

---

## Constitution Manifest
Your endurance stabilizes your form.
### Hardened Frame
When you roll a Hit Die you regain additional hit points equal to your proficiency bonus.
### Steady Pulse
You have advantage on Constitution saves against exhaustion.

---

## Intelligence Manifest
Your cognition accelerates and stabilizes.
### Analytical Recall
You gain proficiency in one Intelligence-based skill of your choice.
### Logical Defense
You gain advantage on one Intelligence saving throw per long rest.

---

## Wisdom Manifest
Your perception aligns with reality.
### Heightened Awareness
You gain proficiency in Perception or Insight.
### Grounded Mind
You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened once per long rest.

---

## Charisma Manifest
Your presence asserts itself without effort.
### Commanding Tone
You gain proficiency in Persuasion or Intimidation.
### Social Anchor
You can reroll one failed Charisma check per long rest.

---

## Aeronaut Pilot
*Prerequisite: Vehicle proficiency or training*
You are trained to command aerial vessels.
### Aerial Vehicles
You gain proficiency with airships and zeppelins.
### Emergency Handling
When piloting an airship you have advantage on checks to avoid collisions crashes or loss of control.

## Veil-Habituated
You have adapted to prolonged exposure to the Veil.
### Veil Mitigation
Environmental hazards from the Veil are treated as one step less severe.
### Rest Assimilation
Short rests taken in the Veil restore one additional Hit Die.
### Veil Sense
You sense thinning or breaches in the Veil within 30 feet.

---

## Iterative Reasoning
You solve problems through refinement and reassessment.
### Refined Attempt
After failing an Intelligence check you may retry after 1 minute using a different approach.
### False Assumption
On a success the DM reveals one misleading factor avoided.

---

## Productive Error
Failure produces insight rather than loss.
### Degraded Success
When you fail a tool check by 5 or less you achieve a temporary or degraded result.
### Learning Curve
You gain advantage on the next check with the same tool within 1 hour.

---

## Conversational Inertia
You understand how conversations accumulate momentum.
### Momentum Gain
After a successful Charisma check the next related Charisma check in the scene has advantage.
### Soft Failure
After a failed Charisma check hostile escalation requires an additional trigger.

---

## Improvised Standardization
You normalize chaos and substandard conditions.
### Field Normalization
Improvised or poor tool setups count as proper equipment.
### Emergency Repair
You can restore a broken tool to limited function without materials.

---

## Operational Planner
You think in supply chains and preparation.
### Reduced Setup
During downtime reduce group travel or setup time by 25 percent.
### Supply Buffer
Once per journey negate one resource depletion event.

---

## Parallel Focus
You divide attention between overlapping demands.
### Split Focus
You may concentrate on one spell and one non-spell effect simultaneously.
### Unstable Focus
Failing a concentration save ends only one effect chosen randomly.

---

## Preemptive Read
You read intent before action.
### Early Read
At the start of combat ask one yes-or-no question about enemy capabilities. Answer must be truthful but minimal.

---

## Maker’S Signature
Your work carries identity and consequence.
### Recognizable Craft
Items you create are identifiable as yours.
### Reputation Weight
NPC reactions shift based on familiarity with your work.

---

## Ordonnant Scarred
You were altered by contact with higher law.
### Rule Enforcement
Once per day declare an outcome invalid if it contradicts an established rule previously demonstrated in play.

---

## Environmental Calibration
You rapidly adapt to unfamiliar or hostile terrain.
### Adaptive Posture
After 10 minutes in a new environment you ignore one terrain-based penalty.
### Situational Memory
You have advantage on checks to navigate or operate in that environment for the next 24 hours.

---

## Deferred Impact
You act with delayed intent.
### Held Consequence
Once per combat you may declare that a successful attack or effect resolves at the end of the target’s next turn instead of immediately.
### Pressure Window
Until it resolves the target has disadvantage on its next saving throw.

---

## Cognitive Offload
You externalize mental strain.
### Stress Buffer
When you fail a mental saving throw you may delay the effect until the end of your next turn.
### Mental Triage
If you use this feature you have disadvantage on Intelligence checks until your next short rest.

---

## Situational Authority
Your confidence fills uncertainty.
### Command the Moment
When no clear leader is present you gain advantage on one Charisma check to direct a group.
### Stabilize Crowd
Once per long rest you can prevent a neutral crowd from turning hostile.

---

## Material Intuition
You understand matter by touch and failure.
### Read the Grain
After handling an object you learn one property relevant to crafting or breaking it.
### Waste Reduction
Failed crafting attempts consume half the usual materials.

---

## Momentum Banking
You convert success into future advantage.
### Stored Momentum
When you succeed on a check by 5 or more you may store momentum.
### Release Momentum
Before your next long rest you may spend it to gain advantage on a related check.

---

## Boundary Sense
You feel the edges of reality.
### Planar Tension
You always know when you are within 60 feet of a planar boundary or weakened veil.
### Stability Check
You have advantage on saves against forced teleportation.

---

## Operational Redundancy
You plan for failure as a certainty.
### Fallback Plan
Once per long rest when a plan fails the group may immediately attempt an alternate approach without time loss.
### Distributed Load
Exhaustion from travel affects one fewer creature of your choice.

---

## Narrative Anchoring
You remember what should not change.
### Anchor Event
Once per arc you may declare one established fact immune to retcon or contradiction.
### Continuity Sense
You immediately notice when events diverge from established truth.

---

## Witness Of Collapse
You have seen systems fail.
### Predict Failure
Once per day you may ask the DM what element of a situation is most likely to fail first.
### Prepared Response
Your first reaction to that failure has advantage.
